Constructs a FundraisingCampaign instance.

id is the unique identifier of the campaign. fundId is the identifier of the fund to which the campaign belongs. name is the name of the campaign. startDate is the start date of the campaign. endDate is the end date of the campaign. currency is the currency used for the campaign. pledges is a list of pledge identifiers associated with the campaign. pledgedAmount is the total amount pledged to the campaign. goalAmount is the funding goal of the campaign.
